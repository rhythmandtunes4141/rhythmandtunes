Prof. Madan Prasad

Prof. Madan Prasad is a renowned vocalist of the Gwalior Gharana with over 30 years of experience in the field of Indian classical music. He has been associated with Mumbai University for the past 10 years.

He has performed extensively across India as well as internationally.

Achievements:
  • Sur Sangeet bhushan puraskar-2012
  • Gungaurav puraskar -2016 by Governer Ram Naik
  • Samajratna puraskar-2023

Prof. Prasad is also an accomplished music composer and a disciple of Late Pt. Yashavant Deo, Pt. Prabhakar Dhakade, and Anil Mohile.

He was closely associated with Late Rajeev Dixit during the Swadeshi Movement. Inspired by this association, he continues to actively lead and promote the Swadeshi Movement across the country.
